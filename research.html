Types of AI

There are four different main types of AI.

Type One: Reactive Machines

These are the simplest machines, they do not have the capabilities to use past experiances to influence decisions, they are (hence the name) purely reactive. For example an early AI model that could play chess based of probabilty and single choice making was invented in 1990, it did not have much capability to take into account the future and effects of its decision. Reactive machines are a basic machine that is more Artificial than Intelligent when compared to the other types, it is not as smart or intelligent however very simple to create and implement therefore it is still classed as a relevant model for some projects and needs. They do not learn or adapt to future scenarios based of data and past experiances, they simply make a decision based of what is given to them.
